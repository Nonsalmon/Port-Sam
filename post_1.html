Tic-Tac-Toe App

This is practice work, not for a competition. In my work, I created for developing mobile apps on the Android operating system and attempt to develop a mobile application. The tools for development are Android Studio and Java. The concept is that there are two players X and O. If a player scores three times in a row horizontally or vertically, or diagonally, that player is the winner. This application's unique feature is that I designed a full-screen format for players.Improve for having a good experience for gameplay.This work has taught me how important it is to be precise when naming functions and arranging elements to keep from making a mistake.
